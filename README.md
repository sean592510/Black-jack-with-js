- Visual card display with suits and ranks
- Win/lose/draw detection
- Score tracking (Player & Dealer)
- Responsive design ‚Äì works on desktop and mobile
- Clean, minimal UI with smooth animations

---

## How to Play

1. Click **"New Game"** to start.
2. You are dealt two cards. The dealer gets one face-up card.
3. **Hit** to take another card, or **Stand** to pass.
4. Try to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.
5. Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10 points. Ace = 1 or 11.
